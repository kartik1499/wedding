Our Love Story

The first meet

We were forced to take a pre-requisite discrete math class where a mutual friend introduced us. In one of the lectures, I was trying to solve a coding question and she had the right solution and I was blown away!

In Love

After our first introduction, I knew I wanted to know more about that shy guy in class! Lectures and assignments became more fun together, and before we knew it we started spending days together, enjoying each other’s company and falling in love. Officially, we decided we wanted to be together over a midnight ice cream celebration after completing a project

The journey together

The ride was bumpy, and I know our friends will vouch for that, but we learned early on to be part of one team. Be it Kartik prepping me for my coding interviews, or me giving him the confidence that he always needed, we knew we were always better together. Going through that, we found jobs in 2 different cities- Austin and San Francisco. While I was closer to home Kartik had it tougher, but our resolve to be together never quivered.

The Wedding

I knew I wanted to marry Aneri very early in what most people would call our dating period. To me, we never really conventionally dated. We must’ve gone on 2, maybe 3 dates. But we were there for each other when we needed to be. And that’s when I knew she was the one. We’re now having our big fat Indian wedding, and I couldn’t be happier that I get to spend the rest of my life with her!
